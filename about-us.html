Our Mission

Sunrise Financial brings affordable solar to homeowners who have been shut out by traditional lenders due to low income, limited credit, or nonstandard financial situations.

We believe clean energy should improve a family's financial stability, not depend on it.

Leadership Team

[Photo: Thomas Neyhart]

Thomas A. Neyhart

Chief Executive Officer

Thomas Neyhart is a nationally recognized clean-energy entrepreneur with more than two decades of leadership in residential solar, financing, and energy-efficiency access. He has helped finance over $1 billion in solar installations and raised more than $1.4 billion in investment capital to expand affordable solar to tens of thousands of working families.

He was named a Goldman Sachs Top 100 Entrepreneur and is widely known for pioneering programs focused on low-to-moderate-income (LMI) households.

Guiding Principle: Energy Savings for Every Family.

Reid Garton

Chief Strategy Officer

Reid Garton leads corporate strategy, analytics, data systems, and market expansion. He previously helped scale a national clean-energy platform to more than 200,000 customers across 14 states, building the data-driven infrastructure required for large-scale, sustainable growth.

Reid ensures Sunrise Financial stays aligned with its mission while deploying technology and strategy that maximize impact and profitability.

Lisa Neyhart

Chief Experience Officer

Lisa Neyhart oversees the full customer journey — from first conversation to 25 years of service.
